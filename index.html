FIRST Tech Challenge

The FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) is a robotics competition program designed for middle and high school students. It challenges and promote hands-on STEM learning, problem-solving, and teamwork. FTC aims to inspire a passion for science and technology among the young minds, preparing them for future careers in STEM fields while emphasizing values like gracious professionalism and collaboration.
