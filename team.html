Meet Our Team

Our team is varied. Programming members are experienced: some of them started to programme by primary school, and have experiences of computing thousands of codes. The engineering and constructing members are skillful as well. Creative ideas together with diligent working attitudes construct teeny parts into a giant robot with well performance. We also have artistic teammates. They come up with beautiful paintings and moving words to bring more colorness toward our team. The project manager team can't be forgotten as well. Their responsibility as well as their kindness makes this team organized and stronger.

About
Treeman 19600 Team

The treeman 19600 team represents YK Pao High School, and have been one of the strongest teams in Shanghai, China.

Joining the FIRST Tech Challenge competition for over 6 years, Treeman 19600 team has won a range of awards. Check out our achievements here.

We have excellent members participating in engineering projects, programming projects, outreaching and project managing.
